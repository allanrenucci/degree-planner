Degree Planner is an application that allows a student to plan his coursework from a degree-wide perspective of filling all the requisites of the school and the program

Degree Planner allows the user to:

  • Create new schools, programs, faculties and classes.
  • Arrange the class requirements into class groups and categories in a very flexible way.
  • Decide which classes to take with instant feedback on the impact towards the filling of requirements.
  • Share all that was created with other users from the same school for easier future builds.
  • Add grades for completed classes and produce shareable and styled gradesheets.
